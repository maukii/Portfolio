Info

I'm currently working @ Small Giant Games on Empires & Puzzles

Since 2016, I've been exploring the digital landscapes as a Game Developer & Programmer.
My chosen weapons of choice? Unity and C#, which I've wielded with expertise to bring gaming fantasies to life.
From crafting mobile free-to-play games to embarking on epic quests to bring gaming joy to the Steam platform, I've ventured into diverse realms.
Among my proudest achievements is the transformation of a free-to-play mobile game into a full-fledged Steam release, complete with controller support.
It was like leveling up a character, but in the real world.



Let's delve into the tech side of my tale. In the realm of real-time gaming, I've donned the mantle of a programming wizard.
Unity is my trusty steed, but I'm known to tame Unreal when the occasion calls for it.
I've dabbled in the arcane arts of shaders, mastered the intricacies of optimization, and made user interfaces sparkle like rare in-game items.
My journey hasn't been confined to a single path.
I've tread both front-end and back-end development, forging new content and features that beckon players to return for more adventures.
